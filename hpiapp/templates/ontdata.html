Ontario Dwellings Data

Click on the legend to explore key markets in Ontario by dwelling type.





The marker clusters represent average benchmark prices for various housing types in Ontario. You can compare the average price of each dwelling to the composite for real-time data on which dwelling type is most profitable in these markets.

The regions included in this analysis are Barrie and District, Greater Toronto Area, Guelph, Hamilton and Burlington, Niagara Region, Oakville and Milton, and Ottawa.

The data represents housing prices from January 1st 2018 to January 31st 2019.
